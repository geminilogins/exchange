What is the Gemini exchange?

Wondering what this exchange is and how it could help us in giving us a safe and seamless trading experience? Well, this is a privately-owned exchange that lets users buy, sell and trade more than 40 types of cryptocurrency. And, that's not all; this exchange allows the users to store all those cryptos that they have purchased over time.

Launched in the year 2015, Gemini exchange has been winning the hearts of users by providing them a safe and convenient experience. At the time when it was launched by Cameron and Tyler Winklevoss, they did not know that this exchange would gain so much reputation and popularity over time. However, as time passed by, this exchange gained a lot of new users with each passing day. And, today, it is so popular that it is giving a tough competition to some other popular exchanges such as Coinbase, Coinmama, and Kraken.

How do you create an account on the Gemini exchange?

After learning a great deal about Gemini exchange, let us try to have a look at the process to create an account on Gemini:

  1. The first thing that you need to do here is to go to gemini.com

  2. You can do this from a mobile or PC web browser

  3. Next up, head to the top-right corner of the page and click "Register"

  4. This shall open a page where you have to enter these details one by one:

  • Full name

  • The email address that is actively being used

  • Choose a password

  1. When you are done filling in these details, read the User Agreement and Privacy Policy

  2. If you agree to these terms, check the box for showing your agreement

  3. After this, click on the link labeled as "Create my account"

  4. Then comes the page where you need to enter your current phone number

  5. Now, you will be sent a code by team Gemini which you need to enter in the given space

  6. Then, you have to link a bank account to make the purchases

  7. After that, verify your identity by completing the KYC process

  8. Thereafter, Gemini will ask you to upload your Photo ID and proof of address docs

After completing this process, you may need to wait for about three business days to get the verification completed. Thus, it is more than important to have patience at this point, and then you can go ahead with trading on the Gemini exchange.
